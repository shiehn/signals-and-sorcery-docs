## You compose — AI generates the layers

Define a musical contract, then generate infinite MIDI and audio layers within it — MIDI via **Gemini**, audio via **Lyria**. Instead of one-shot generation that hands you a finished song, Signals & Sorcery keeps you in the creative process: build, preview, and perform with full creative control.
